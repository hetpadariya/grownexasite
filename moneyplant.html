Money Plant

About the Plant

Money Plant is a fast-growing indoor vine known for attracting positivity and purifying air. It grows easily with very little care and survives well even in low light conditions.

How to Grow

  • Can be grown in soil or water.
  • Use well-drained soil in a pot with drainage holes.
  • Add stick or wall support if you want long vines.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Prefers bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Keep near a window for best growth.
  • Avoid direct sunlight — leaves may burn.

Water Requirement

  • Water 2 times a week.
  • Let soil dry 30–40% before watering again.
  • If growing in water, change water every 7–10 days.
