Jade Plant

About the Plant

Jade Plant is a popular succulent known for its thick, shiny, smooth leaves. It is considered a good luck plant and is very easy to care for.

How to Grow

  • Use well-draining soil (cactus or succulent mix).
  • Choose a small pot with good drainage.
  • Do not overwater — Jade stores water in leaves.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Needs 4–6 hours of indirect sunlight.
  • Can grow indoors near a bright window.

Water Requirement

  • Water once every 10–15 days.
  • Let the soil dry completely between waterings.
  • Overwatering may cause roots to rot.
