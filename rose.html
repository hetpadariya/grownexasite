Rose

About the Plant

Roses are one of the most popular flowering plants in the world. They grow in many colors and produce beautiful, fragrant blooms. They prefer outdoor bright sunlight and fertile soil.

How to Grow

  • Use rich, well-drained soil mixed with compost.
  • Plant in a large pot or directly in garden soil.
  • Prune dead stems regularly to encourage new flowers.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Needs 4–6 hours of direct sunlight daily.
  • Morning sunlight is best for healthy blooming.

Water Requirement

  • Water every 2–3 days.
  • Keep soil moist but never soggy.
  • In summer, watering daily may be needed.
