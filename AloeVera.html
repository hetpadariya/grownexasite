Aloe Vera

About the Plant

Aloe Vera is a medicinal succulent plant known for its healing gel. It grows easily in dry conditions and requires very little water.

How to Grow

  • Use sandy or well-drained soil for best growth.
  • Plant in a medium-sized pot with drainage holes.
  • Avoid overwatering — Aloe prefers dry soil.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Needs bright sunlight for at least 4–6 hours daily.
  • Can grow indoors near a sunny window.

Water Requirement

  • Water once every 7–10 days.
  • Let the soil dry completely before watering again.
  • Overwatering may cause root rot.
