Boston Fern

About the Plant

Boston Fern is a beautiful indoor plant known for its long arching fronds. It is great for improving air quality and adds a lush green look to homes.

How to Grow

  • Use rich, well-draining soil that holds moisture.
  • Keep the plant in a humid area like bathroom or balcony.
  • Mist the leaves regularly for healthy growth.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Prefers bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Avoid strong direct sunlight — fronds may dry out.

Water Requirement

  • Keep soil moist at all times.
  • Water 3–4 times a week depending on heat.
  • Do not let soil dry completely.
