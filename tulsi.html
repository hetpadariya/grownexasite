Tulsi

About the Tulsi Plant

Tulsi is a sacred and medicinal plant commonly grown in Indian homes. It is loved for its strong aroma, health benefits, and ability to purify the air. With proper sunlight and care, it grows quickly and becomes bushy.

How to Grow

  • Use light, well-draining soil.
  • Plant seeds or a small sapling in a medium pot with drainage holes.
  • Pinch the top leaves regularly to encourage bushy growth.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Needs 4–5 hours of direct sunlight every day.
  • Best kept near a balcony or sunny window.

Water Requirement

  • Water every 1–2 days depending on temperature.
  • Keep soil slightly moist — not too dry, not soggy.
  • Avoid overwatering because Tulsi roots rot easily.
