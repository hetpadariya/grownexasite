Spider Plant

About the Plant

Spider Plant is a low-maintenance indoor plant known for its long ribbon-like leaves. It purifies air naturally and is perfect for beginners.

How to Grow

  • Use light, well-draining soil.
  • Plant in a medium pot with drainage holes.
  • Trim brown tips to keep the plant fresh.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Prefers bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Can survive in low-light conditions.

Water Requirement

  • Water 2–3 times a week.
  • Keep soil slightly moist.
  • Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
