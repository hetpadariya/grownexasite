Snake Plant

About the Plant

Snake Plant is a hardy indoor plant known for its tall, sword-like leaves. It is excellent for air purification and grows easily with very little care.

How to Grow

  • Use well-drained soil (cactus mix works best).
  • Choose a pot that is not too big.
  • Repot only when roots become tight.

Sunlight Requirement

  • Can grow in low, medium, or bright light.
  • Best growth in indirect sunlight.

Water Requirement

  • Water once every 10–14 days.
  • Let the soil dry completely before watering again.
  • Very drought-tolerant—do not overwater.
